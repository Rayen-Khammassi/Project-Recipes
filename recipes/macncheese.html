Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese is a classic comfort food loved by many. This recipe will guide you through making a creamy and cheesy version at home.

Ingredients

  • 200g macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Combine the cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce, ensuring the pasta is well coated.
  6. Serve hot, and enjoy your homemade mac and cheese!
