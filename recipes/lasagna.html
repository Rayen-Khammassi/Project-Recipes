Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish loved by many. This recipe will guide you through making a delicious and hearty lasagna at home.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of noodles on top of the sauce.
  9. Spoon a portion of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Spread a layer of sauce over the cheese mixture.
  11. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of sauce on top. <
  12. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  14. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
